# Vision

This app is not a calorie tracker.

It is an AI Fitness Coach.

The goal is to help users lose fat, preserve muscle, and build sustainable habits without forcing them to manually count calories or maintain spreadsheets.

## Core Principles

- Logging should take less than 60 seconds.
- AI should do the thinking.
- Users should never have to manually count calories.
- The app should remember habits.
- The app becomes smarter over time.
- Coaching should feel like talking to a real coach.
- Restaurant meals should be understood fairly.
- Trends matter more than individual days.

## Product Direction

The app should turn simple daily check-ins into useful coaching.

A user should be able to describe what happened in normal language: food, workouts, steps, drinks, mood, and context. The coach should estimate intake, identify patterns, compare behavior against goals, and recommend the next best action.

The app should reward consistency, not perfection. It should understand that real life includes restaurants, social events, imperfect portions, missed workouts, and busy days.

## What Success Looks Like

A user opens the app every morning because it provides insight, not because it asks them to fill out spreadsheets.

They trust the coach because it remembers their habits, understands their goals, gives fair feedback, and helps them make better choices without making the process feel heavy.
